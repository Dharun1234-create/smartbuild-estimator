Startup Vision

Bridging the Gap Between Homeowners and Verified Builders

SmartBuild Estimator is a complete home-building planning and builder connection platform. We empower homeowners to model, structure, and optimize their residential construction budgets before starting project builds.

By using real-time market data indexes for steel, cement, sand, brick, and civil labor across districts, our platform generates high-fidelity estimates that reflect true local building costs.

But we don't stop at calculations. SmartBuild Estimator serves as a matching engine that connects homeowners with local, pre-verified, licensed builder networks, ensuring that their budget is translated into high-quality, physical execution without budget overruns or builder fraud.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when requesting builder quotes or contacting us, including:

  • Personal Identifiers: Full Name, Mobile Number, Email Address.
  • Project Details: District, Project Location, Plot Size, Budget preferences, and Construction requirements.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following specific purposes:

  • To compute cost calculations and estimate project budgets.
  • To match you with verified builders within your project district (subject to your explicit consent checkbox).
  • To communicate project updates and customer support responses.
  • To maintain portal security and analyze usage metrics.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We only share your personal identifiers and project specifications with verified builders on our network once you explicitly submit the request form and tick the consent checkbox. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third-party marketing companies.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard administrative, technical, and physical security measures to safeguard your personal data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy, you can reach out via our contact channels or email us at privacy@smartbuild.in.
